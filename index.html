About ArtVista Museum

Founded in 1925, ArtVista Museum is one of the world's leading art museums, housing a vast collection that spans 5,000 years of human creativity from around the globe.

Our mission is to collect, preserve, study, exhibit, and stimulate appreciation for works of art that collectively represent the broadest spectrum of human achievement.

The museum's collection includes more than 2 million works of art, from ancient sculptures to contemporary installations, making it one of the most comprehensive art museums in existence.
