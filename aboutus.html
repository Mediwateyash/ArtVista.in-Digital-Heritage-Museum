About the Ancient Shivaji Maharaj Museum

Preserving the legacy of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the visionary Maratha warrior king.

Our History

Established in 1980, the Ancient Shivaji Maharaj Museum was founded to honor the life and achievements of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire. The museum houses over 5,000 artifacts, including weapons, manuscripts, and royal attire from the 17th century.

Located in Pune, the cultural heart of Maharashtra, the museum serves as an educational hub for historians, students, and visitors worldwide.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To preserve and promote the rich heritage of the Maratha Empire through immersive exhibits, research, and public engagement.

Vision

To become a global center for Maratha history, inspiring future generations with Shivaji Maharaj's values of leadership and justice.

Values

Integrity, education, and cultural preservation guide every exhibit and program we create.
